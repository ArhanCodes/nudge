# Nudge

> "To what extent can a personalised digital tool influence individual sustainable behaviour change across multiple environmental domains?"


### What it does
Nudge lets users track their daily habits across four sustainability domains (transport, diet, energy use, and waste). Each activity is converted into a real CO₂e score using emissions data, giving users a clear picture of their environmental footprint over time.

### Features

- daily habit logging across transport, diet, energy and waste
- carbon footprint calculator using real emissions data (kg CO2e)
- personalised weekly tips based on your worst-performing category
- progress dashboard with charts showing improvement over time
- streak and badge system to keep you consistent
- onboarding flow — guided 4-step setup for new users
- 12 region-specific CO2 factors — adjusts energy estimates based on your country's electricity grid
- national benchmark comparisons — shows how you compare to your country's average (with BEIS/DEFRA/EPA sources)
- goal progress ring — visual circular tracker for your weekly CO2 target
- CSV data export — download your logs for analysis
- daily push notification reminders at 8pm
- streak grace period warning — reminds you to log before you lose your streak
